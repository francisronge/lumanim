# Mission: Paradoxes

## Why
Test whether Lumanim can make a philosophical tension perceptible—not merely describe it—while preserving the complete `/teach` learning loop.

## Success looks like
- Experience the Ship of Theseus as two competing identity claims.
- Make and reconsider a prediction using a real ManimGL visual.
- Use one HTML lesson whether or not the live runtime is available.

## Constraints
- This is a disposable first proof, not a broad philosophy curriculum.
- The learner-facing surface is HTML.
- Real 3b1b/manim ManimGL is mandatory.

## Out of scope
- Surveying proposed solutions to personal identity.
- Establishing a single correct answer to the paradox.
