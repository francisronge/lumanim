# Ship of Theseus Resources

## Knowledge

- [Plutarch, *Life of Theseus*, 23.1 — Perseus Digital Library](https://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus%3Atext%3A2008.01.0067%3Achapter%3D23%3Asection%3D1)
  Primary ancient passage: the Athenians replaced decaying timbers and philosophers disputed whether the vessel remained the same.
- [“Identity Over Time” — Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/identity-time/)
  Scholarly account of the harder replacement-and-reassembly case, where two later ships each appear related to the original.
